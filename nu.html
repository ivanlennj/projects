Welcome toNkumba University

Nkumba University (NU) is a private non-for-profit University that has currently positioned itself as a one stop centre for cutting edge, high-quality education and research within the country and the region. Nkumba University has positioned itself at the forefront of supporting the government in skilling its citizens through undertaking cutting edge research and providing training on courses that are of importance and are required for economic development.

The university offers several programmes leading to the award of certificates, diplomas, degrees as well as postgraduate academic qualifications. The university has so far graduated out over 25,000 students who are part of the alumni occupying several important roles in Uganda and the rest of the world.

Nkumba University

Nkumba University was chartered by the government of Uganda in 2006 and from that time it has continued to impact on its community through education and community engagement. NU is committed to achieving this vision through an enriched learning experience that blends a variety of teaching and learning methods.
